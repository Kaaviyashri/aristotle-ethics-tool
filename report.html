Assessment Reports

Review and download your ARISTOTLE Assessment results

Report Overview

Each assessment you complete in ARISTOTLE can be exported as a PDF report that includes your role, responses, and summary findings.

After completing an assessment, use the "Generate PDF Report" button on the results page to download your comprehensive evaluation document.

How to Access Your Reports

1. Complete Assessment

Navigate to the Assessment page and complete all questions for your selected role.

2. View Results

After submitting, you'll see your ethical readiness score and recommendations.

3. Generate PDF

Click "Generate PDF Report" to download a comprehensive document with all details.
