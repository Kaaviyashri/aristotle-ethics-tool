About ARISTOTLE

ARISTOTLE (AI Readiness and Integrated System for Tracking Oversight, Trust, and Lawful Ethics) is a self-assessment and learning framework designed to help clinicians, developers, and policy leaders evaluate and strengthen the ethical trustworthiness of healthcare AI systems based on the six WHO principles: Autonomy, Safety, Transparency, Accountability, Fairness, and Privacy.

Each user receives:

  • Real-time readiness scores across ethical domains
  • Visual dashboards showing strengths and risks
  • Downloadable PDF reports for education and compliance tracking
